Biochemistry Mnemonics

"Great Grandmother Throws Fresh Fruit Dish And Goes By Picking Pumpkins Pronto"

"LEO the lion says GER: Loss of Electrons is Oxidation, Gain of Electrons is Reduction"

"Fatty Liver Mnemonic: 'FAT RATS' - Fasting, Alcohol, Toxins, Refeeding, Acute fatty liver, TPN, Starvation"

Pathology Mnemonics

"HAMARTOMAS" mnemonic for tuberous sclerosis features.

"ABCDEF" for melanoma risk factors: Asymmetry, Border, Color, Diameter, Evolution, Funny looking

"NAGGING P" for chronic inflammation: Neoplasia, Autoimmune, Granuloma, Infection, Non-infectious, Genetic, Persistent

Pharmacology Mnemonics

"A BEAM" for beta-blockers (Atenolol, Bisoprolol, Esmolol, Acebutolol, Metoprolol).

"SLUDGE" for cholinergic toxicity: Salivation, Lacrimation, Urination, Diarrhea, GI upset, Emesis

"ABCDE" for managing poisoning: Airway, Breathing, Circulation, Decontamination, Enhanced elimination

Physiology Mnemonics

"ABCDE" for primary survey in trauma.

"CALCIUM" for hypercalcemia causes: Cancer, Addison's, Lithium, Calcium excess, Immobilization, Unusual parathyroid, Milk-alkali

"RAAS" for renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system: Renin, Angiotensin, Aldosterone, Sodium

Microbiology Mnemonics

"Some Nasty Bugs May Live FacultativeLY" for facultative intracellular bacteria.

"BE SPOT" for encapsulated bacteria: Bacteroides, E. coli, Streptococcus, Pseudomonas, Haemophilus, Klebsiella

"Never Let Monkeys Eat Bananas" for WBC differential: Neutrophils, Lymphocytes, Monocytes, Eosinophils, Basophils
